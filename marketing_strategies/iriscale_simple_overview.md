## What Is Iriscale?

Iriscale is an all-in-one AI marketing platform that helps companies accelerate their go-to-market strategy. Instead of juggling multiple tools for keyword research, AI Optimization, SEO, content planning, competitive analysis, and social media—Iriscale brings everything together in one intelligent system that learns your business and creates content that stays on-brand.

**The core idea:** Add your company information once, and AI handles the rest—from discovering what your customers are searching for to creating ready-to-publish articles and social posts.
