# Iriscale Product Features - Marketing Overview

A comprehensive marketing guide to Iriscale's major product features, highlighting key capabilities and customer benefits.

---

## Intelligence & Research

### 1. Knowledge Base
**Description:** AI-powered company intelligence hub that automatically extracts and structures data from websites, documents, YouTube videos, and competitor URLs to create a unified "source of truth" for all content generation.

**Customer Benefit:** Upload your materials once and let AI do the heavy lifting—all generated content stays on-brand, fact-based, and consistent without manual data entry.

---

### 2. Keyword Repository
**Description:** Centralized keyword management hub using sophisticated AI with Google APIs to generate comprehensive keyword sets. Includes search volume, competition metrics, CPC data, and product/service linking.

**Customer Benefit:** Get world-class keyword research without the guesswork—AI discovers keywords your competitors target and links them directly to your products for targeted campaigns.

---

### 3. Search Ranking Intelligence
**Description:** AI-powered monitoring tool that queries all major AI search providers (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok) simultaneously to track company visibility and competitive positioning.

**Customer Benefit:** Know exactly where your company ranks across AI search platforms and discover opportunities to improve visibility before your competitors do.

---

### 4. Competitor Analysis
**Description:** Automated competitive intelligence that generates comprehensive comparison reports with feature matrices, positioning analysis, sales battle cards, and partnership assessments from competitor data.

**Customer Benefit:** Get deep competitive insights automatically—including ready-to-use sales battlecards and strategic recommendations—without hours of manual research.

---

## Strategy & Planning

### 5. Content Architecture
**Description:** AI-powered website content planning tool that generates a complete hierarchical page structure with SEO settings, link strategies, and content templates. Includes built-in analytics and social promotion.

**Customer Benefit:** Get a strategic content blueprint for your entire website in minutes—complete with SEO optimization and integrated publishing workflows.

---

### 6. Topic Strategy
**Description:** Full-funnel content ideation engine that transforms a single core idea into coordinated topic clusters spanning awareness (TOFU), consideration (MOFU), and decision (BOFU) stages.

**Customer Benefit:** Turn one idea into a complete content plan that guides customers through every stage of their buying journey.

---

### 7. AI Optimization Questions
**Description:** AI-powered question discovery that identifies what customers are asking AI systems like ChatGPT about your keywords, with automated answer generation and content placement.

**Customer Benefit:** Capture the growing AI search market by discovering and answering the exact questions prospects are asking AI assistants.

---

## Content Creation

### 8. Articles
**Description:** Content management hub for AI-generated articles with approval workflows. Articles are created from Content Architecture pages with built-in quality control and bulk management.

**Customer Benefit:** Generate professional, on-brand articles at scale with human oversight—no more staring at blank pages.

---

### 9. AI Optimization Answers
**Description:** AI-generated answer management system that creates optimized responses to customer questions, automatically placing them in the best content architecture location.

**Customer Benefit:** Turn customer questions into published website content automatically—capturing search traffic from AI-driven queries.

---

## Opportunity & Engagement

### 10. Opportunity Agent
**Description:** AI-powered social monitoring system that scans Reddit and other platforms to discover business opportunities, with detailed analysis including blog article recommendations and response drafts.

**Customer Benefit:** Never miss a sales opportunity—AI finds prospects discussing your solution space and gives you ready-made responses to engage them.

---

## Social & Promotion

### 11. Social Posts
**Description:** AI-powered social content generator that creates platform-optimized posts from articles or URLs, with campaign management, strategy briefs, audience targeting, and real-time mobile previews.

**Customer Benefit:** Generate scroll-stopping social content for any platform in seconds—personalized to your brand voice and optimized for engagement.

---

### 12. Social Connections
**Description:** Unified authentication hub supporting 7 major social platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit) with OAuth-based secure connections and team sharing.

**Customer Benefit:** Connect all your social accounts once and empower your entire team to publish—without sharing passwords.

---

### 13. Social Scheduler
**Description:** Publishing command center for managing post lifecycles with status tracking (draft, scheduled, published, cancelled, failed), approval workflows, and scheduling capabilities.

**Customer Benefit:** Plan, approve, and schedule your entire social calendar from one dashboard—never miss a post again.

---

## Account & Collaboration

### 14. Multi-Tenant Organization Management
**Description:** Enterprise-ready architecture supporting multiple organizations and projects with role-based permissions (Owner, Manager, Employee) and centralized team administration.

**Customer Benefit:** Manage multiple brands, clients, or business units from one login with proper access controls for your team.

---

### 15. Guided Onboarding (Feature Exploration)
**Description:** Interactive dashboard with progress tracking that guides new users through all 13 platform features with contextual help, step-by-step instructions, and gamified completion incentives.

**Customer Benefit:** Get up and running fast with guided walkthroughs—no training required to master the platform.

---

## Brand & Internal Guidelines

### 16. Brand Voice & Writing Guidelines
**Description:** Auto-generated writing guidelines that ensure all AI-created content matches your company's tone, voice, and style—with pre-publish checklists and format specifications.

**Customer Benefit:** Every piece of content sounds like your brand wrote it—consistent voice across all channels, every time.

---

### 17. Branding Guidelines
**Description:** Automatically extracted visual identity documentation including color palettes, typography, logos, and design system specifications from your existing brand assets.

**Customer Benefit:** Keep all AI-generated visual content perfectly on-brand without manual style guides.
