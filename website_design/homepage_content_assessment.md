### 2. Hero Section

**Label:** `MARKETING INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM`

**Headline:**

**Subheadline:**  
> "One platform replaces your entire marketing stack. Expert strategy built in. Your brand voice locked in. Content that converts, created in minutes instead of days."

**CTAs:** Try for Free | Book Demo

**Questions:**
- [ ] Is "Marketing Intelligence Platform" the right category label? Does this resonate with target buyers or is it too generic?

- Consider A/B testing "Marketing Intelligence Platform" vs. more specific labels like "AI Marketing Automation" or "Marketing Strategy Engine"

- Subheadline could be tightened — three separate value props may dilute impact
