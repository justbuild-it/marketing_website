Assessing the Iriscale website design provided from Lunar Branding Agency and comparing it to our features and functions.  See directory iriscale_app_assessment for app feature details.

One of the key concepts and challenges with the marketing website is to explain the value provided to the potential customer for a complicated product without listing every single feature and function. A secondary challenge is that we don't want to diagram every single thing that the product is able to do, out of concern of competitors using our website as a product roadmap.

So there needs to be a balance to help customers understand our value they will get from our product in a very quick and timely manner.
